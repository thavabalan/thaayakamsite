Who we are?

THAAYAKAM LTD, an innovative start-up is found in 2020 with the sole intention to provide a wide range of high-tech products using emerging technologies. We are close knits of tech passionate working towards modern technologies to solve complex real-world problems. Our innovative crew, which works towards new heights provides wide range of solutions and products with a strong commitment. By joining the lightning-fast journey towards the information age that is about to create new worlds with new regulations, THAAYAKAM LTD will take part to explore new era with full of innovation and to build a sustainable education and economic system to aid war torn regions of north east Ceylon Island.

How we born?

The story of us begins with a drastic end of a civil war that happened in the earlier stage of this century in 2009. When the peace talks begin in Ceylon early 2002, The Co-founders of THAAYAKAM LTD were stepped into school and started to explore the new world driven by innovative technology. With the limited access of internet, limited time for Television and Radio and even in a poor level of economy, the interest on exploring the technologies started to explode. As led a successful survival of bloody war that silenced at the end of spring of 2009, the experiences that are learnt and the access to the technologies became easier, Co-founders realized the need of a platform to be built to merge with the ever-changing world and the recovery of economy of war-torn people. Being as friends from school with close bond, the ideas exchanged and THAAYAKAM LTD born to join the lightning-fast journey towards technology driven planet.

Our Future!

By understanding the changes in technologies, global regulations and the environmental habits, THAAYAKAM LTD will continue to get adopt and forge ways to keep up with dynamics of the ever-changing world and to strengthen the economical and educational resources through initiating three level of objectives.

1. Research and Productize innovations to the global market.
2. Ahead of an era without digital divide by increasing the affordability and spread the awareness among the rural population.
3. Help to deliver world class education and build effective economic recovery systems to the war-torn north-east Ceylon.
